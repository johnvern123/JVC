OVERVIEW OF THE CAMPUS LIBRARY

The Bacoor Cavite City Campus Library is located at the Second Floor of the Cavite State University-Bacoor City Campus,
Phase 2, Soldiers Hills IV, Molino VI, Bacoor City, Cavite

CAMPUS LIBRARY VISION

"To promote the humble existence and importance of library in an institution and to its community through quality education where can develop and produce morally upright and competitive individuals"

CAMPUS LIBRARY MISSION

"To support the institution's mission in its commitment that can produce individuals with truth, excellence, and service in any phases and trends of life."

CAMPUS LIBRARY GOALS

1. To provide a conducive place where clientele can stay and study quietly;

2. To respond to the student's multiple academic needs;
